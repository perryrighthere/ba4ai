Use this as a checklist for the **10-minute video** + **500-word executive summary**.

## Deliverables

- Video recording: **10 minutes**, **50%** of module mark.
- Executive summary: **500 words**, **10%** of module mark.

## Video must cover (3 required sections)

1. **Business challenge**
   - deliverables, key levers, scope, and assumptions
2. **Analytics technique application**
   - justify technique choice
   - describe how it was applied
   - describe data + parameters used
3. **Interpretation**
   - translate results into business context
   - recommendations backed by analysis

## Marking breakdown (video, /100)

- Business challenge: **/20**
- Technique application: **/30**
- Interpretation: **/30**
- Presentation: **/20**

## Non-negotiables to include

- **Assumptions**: clearly identified (and used consistently later).
- **Data provenance**: source, date accessed, licence; plus sample size + key features.
- **Reproducibility info**: split method, random seed(s), key parameters/hyperparameters, tool versions.
- **Referencing**: in-slide or final slide; must be clear and correct.
- **GenAI disclosure**: one line stating whether you used AI tools and for what purpose.
- **Time limit**: examiners may stop grading after 10:00.

## Submission deadline (from brief)

- Upload to Blackboard by **12:00 midday on 15 January 2026**.
